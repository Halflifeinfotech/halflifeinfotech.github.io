About Us

Halflife infotech company is a well registered Technology company in Tanzania Under BRELA responsible for performing all tasks dealing with information and technology to meet today's world requirements. The company was established in 2019 By Eng.Shadrack J. Damian (Industrial Engineer) who is now a C.E.O of this Technology Company for the aim of providing solutions to different companies by taking their business to online where 89% of people in the world are available today. In our organization we are dealing with the following;

  • Web Development
  • Graphics Contents Designing and Printing Services
  • Digital Marketing and Business Branding
  • Media Packages Designing.
  • Consultancy in all Technologies Related Matters.

Under this Technology Company we work as a team to make sure we meet all customers specifications and needs,We have our own core values which are Integrity,Responsiveness,Creativity,Innovation,Uniqueness and Smartness in our works. Our slogan states "Pure Design . Pure Technology." which means our Technology is combined together with Creativity which results to wonderful Design that attracts users and enhances the business. Our Vision in the future is to become the largest leading company in Tanzania and Outside the boundaries for Providing most Creative and Innovative Projects that will be solving many challanges facing Business Companies today.
